About the Game

You are an employee at RAYND Corporation. Your job is to look like one. By day, click through reports and keep suspicion low. By night, hack company secrets, build your credits, and manage personas. There's something underneath the network. It knows your name.

Push too far and suspicion rises — audits, interrogations, termination. But push far enough and you get promoted. More access. Deeper systems. A voice in the terminal that knows your name.

Boilerplate

Remain at Your Desk is a cyberpunk incremental clicker where you balance corporate productivity by day with illegal hacking by night. Manage suspicion, survive interrogations, switch personas, and dig through classified data fragments to uncover what RAYND Corporation is really building. 22 upgrades, 6 prestige tiers, and a story that goes deeper than the network. Clock in. Hack out. Remain at your desk.

  • Dual-mode gameplay: corporate productivity by day, illegal hacking by night
  • Deep upgrade tree: 22 upgrades with synergy bonuses, 12 deployable scripts, and 6 prestige tiers
  • Active hacking sequences: route choices, firewall countermeasures, vulnerability windows, and hack streaks
  • Suspicion as a resource: higher risk unlocks rare targets, bonus multipliers, and restricted data
  • Interrogation survival: answer timed questions under corporate scrutiny — your persona determines what gets you fired
  • 6 personas: switch identities to evade pattern detection, but use one too long and it burns
  • Script deployment puzzles: solve 13 logic challenges to unlock powerful automations
  • 18 night events: encrypted transmissions, security sweeps, and choices with real consequences
  • A story that goes somewhere: 10 classified data fragments. Project LIGHTHOUSE. Floor 13. The ORACLE is watching.
