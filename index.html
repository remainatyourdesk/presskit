About the Game

You are Employee #4471. You have been assigned to your desk. You will remain at your desk. RAYND Corporation thanks you for your compliance.

Remain at Your Desk is a darkly comedic cyberpunk incremental game set inside the suffocating infrastructure of a megacorporation that may or may not be watching you right now. Automate your way up the ladder, exploit the system, and wonder why the system feels like it's exploiting you back.

From processing TPS reports to orchestrating full corporate takeovers — the numbers go up, the bureaucracy multiplies, and productivity is mandatory.

Boilerplate

Clock in. Comply. Accumulate. Remain at Your Desk is a cyberpunk incremental clicker where corporate satire meets compulsive automation. Work your way through the RAYND Corporation hierarchy — or let the machine do it for you. Your compliance is appreciated.

  • Dark corporate satire meets incremental game design
  • Automate, delegate, and scale your way up the RAYND hierarchy
  • Offline progression — the corporation runs without you
  • Prestige / restructuring loops for extended replayability
  • Hacking minigame and event-driven narrative moments
  • Original cyberpunk-inflected soundtrack
  • Escalating unlocks: from grunt to architect of the machine
  • Currency hierarchy with meaningful upgrade decisions
  • News ticker flavor text that tells the story of your empire
